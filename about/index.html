    Welcome to Oga's Jamaican Cuisine

    Oga's Jamaican Cuisine is a family owned business. It was started in the kitchen of our home, delivering to Beauty Salons and Barber Shops. We then secured a spot in Bravo's Supermarket's deli section located in South Plainfield. Our name when we initially started was "Yabba's Kitchen."
     
    After building a good reputation and clientele, we moved into our current location where we have both eat-in and take-out options available. We've been fortunate to have been in business for 8 years.
    ​
    Our sincerest thank you goes to our faithful customers for spreading the word about our restaurant and for sticking with us throughout our transition. We started out with the head of our family (Steve aka Yabba) leading the business. After a brief illness, he handed to business over to his wife (Joanne) and his son (Jason) who are still using his authentic style of cooking.
    ​
    We welcome you to come out and try our delicious food! We promise you won't be disappointed.
